Set your timer

Choose your focus time, from 1 half hour to 3 hours, in half hour intervals. Each half hour cycle starts with 25 minutes of "focus" time and ends with 5 minutes of "break" time. If you don't touch your phone during the focus periods, you win! You'll get your buy-in back plus a reward!

Once you win (or if you have to give up), click on the logout at the bottom. But for now, let's get started!

In this beta version, you can choose to demo a work/break transition or a winning situation. Leave the hour set to "0" and choose "win demo" or "break demo" from the minutes dropdown. Wait for one minute to watch the change.
